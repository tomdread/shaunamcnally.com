Infinite Possibilities

The people affected most are in the developing world, far away from the societies that promote scarcity for profit our societies. We celebrate people who sacrifice our world for short-term gains and turn a blind eye to the consequences of our avarice. We have infinite possibilities in front of us, but we choose instead to drown in the waste of our greed and complacency.
