Sunrise

This is my amateur attempt of Cubism I made with scraps of paper & paint. Reflections of trees on water in the pinkish orange glow of the first light surrounded by the retreating blue and purple tones of the previous night.

Theres something about those magical moments of ‘Sunrise that can only be experienced in person but This piece reminds us of that feeling.
